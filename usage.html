How much do teens use social media?

Teenagers and adolescents use social media on average about 3.3 hours a day. A Pew survey during 2014 and 2015 said 94 percent of teens who use a mobile device do so daily. 71 percent of teens say they use more than one social media site.

What are the top social media apps?

Social media apps like Facebook, Instagram, and Snapchat are the most popular.
