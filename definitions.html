Depression-
Feeling extreme sadness, typically also with feelings of not being good enough and guilt, and usually comes with less energy, different appetite and sleeping schedule.
Paranoia-
Includes thoughts of abuse, jealousy, or exaggerated self-importance. It might be a part of chronic personality disorder, a side effect of drug use, or of a serious illness like schizophrenia in which a person confuses reality with their imagination.
ADHD-
Is a chronic condition that includes not being able to focus, hyperactivity, and sometimes acting on impulse. ADHD begins in childhood and usually lasts into adulthood. As many as 2 out of every 3 children with ADHD have symptoms as adults.
